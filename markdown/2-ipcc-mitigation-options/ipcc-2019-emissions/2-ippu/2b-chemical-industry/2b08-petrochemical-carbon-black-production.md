This category can be further broken down for reporting purposes into emissions from:

- `2B8.a` - Methanol
- `2B8.b` - Ethylene
- `2B8.c` - Ethylene Dichloride and Vinyl Chloride Monomer
- `2B8.d` - Ethylene Oxide
- `2B8.e` - Acrylonitrile
- `2B8.f` - Carbon Black
